The Club

The University of Warwick Trampoline Club is a friendly and inclusive club that caters to all abilities, whether you’ve never bounced before or are a national trampolinist. We are always a big presence at competitions around the country with our members competing from novice all the way to the top levels.

We have six trampolines, including one 4x4 trampoline and run sessions five times a week, along with a conditioning session. These sessions are not compulsory and you can pick and choose which ones you want to come to, meaning that we are one of the most flexible clubs on campus!

We also hold weekly socials, which include circling at Pop!, nights out when we go to competitions, meals, bar crawls, trips and our annual tour at Easter.

We travel to many competitions each term and also hold our own annual Warwick Comp. Due to our success at competitions we have managed to achieve a silver Sportmark accreditation in the competition section, as well as bronze in both participation and community.

We are always really excited to welcome new members, and offer free taster sessions in week 1 and 2 of the first term so you can try out trampolining! Make sure you check out our Twitter and Facebook pages for our latest news and if you have any questions then email us – check the contact page for more details!
